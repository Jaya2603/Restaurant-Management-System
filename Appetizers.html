Appetizers




Tacos ₹160

A taco is a traditional Mexican food consisting of a small hand-sized corn- or wheat-based tortilla topped with a filling including beans, vegetables, and cheese, allowing for great versatility and variety.

Corn Toast ₹100

Corn Toast is a delicious Appetizer made with sweet corn and with crisp toast topped with corn and with loads of cheese.

Potato Puff ₹120

Potato Puff, a snack with crisp-n-flaky outer layer and mixed vegetables stuffing, is a quick and tasty way of satisfying urge of something savory.

Tofu Nuggets ₹140

Tofu Nuggets are perfect Appetizer. Tofu is quickly marinated in a mixture of soy sauce and seasonings before being coated in seasoned cornflake crumbs and baked in the oven until nice and crispy.

Panner Tikka ₹200

Paneer tikka is an Indian dish made from chunks of paneer marinated in spices and are then arranged on a stick with capsicums, onions and tomatoes and grilled in a tandoor.

Shami kebab ₹180

Shami kebab is an appetizer made of chickpeas with whole hot spices. These are great to serve with ketchup and mint chutney.
